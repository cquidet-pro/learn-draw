🛠️ Step Editor

Paste the steps I give you (or from the draw tool) into the box, hit Load, then step through with ◀ ▶. Edit any stroke's SVG and the picture updates live. Tweak hints, add/delete strokes & steps, even draw new strokes right on the canvas. Then Copy the result back. Past steps show faded; the current step is bold (just like the app).
