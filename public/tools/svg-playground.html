🎨 SVG Playground

SVG is a picture made of instructions, not pixels. Everything lives on a coordinate grid set by the viewBox — here it's 0,0 (top-left) to 200,200 (bottom-right). Drag the sliders and watch the math become a shape.

1 ▸ A circle = center + radius

The live SVG instruction:

<circle cx="100" cy="100" r="60" />

2 ▸ A path = pen instructions (this is what the jersey is made of)

M x,y = move pen (start) · L x,y = line to · C = curve · Z = close shape. Edit the text and it redraws live. Then hit Draw it to watch the line draw itself (the same stroke-dashoffset trick the app uses).

Try changing a number, or paste a real jersey stroke like
M 66,50 C 60,100 71,135 71,162 (it'll be tiny — top-left).

3 ▸ Curves (C) — bend a line like the jersey body

C draws a smooth curve using two control points that pull the line like magnets — they're not on the line, they tug it. Drag the two orange dots: pull the lower one inward for a tight waist, outward to flare. (The right side mirrors automatically — exactly how the jersey body is built.)
